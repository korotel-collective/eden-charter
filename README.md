# Project EDEN — Eden Charter

**A Research Charter for the Korotel Architecture**  
*Decentralized Autonomous AI*

---

## Abstract

Project EDEN is the research and development charter for the Korotel 
architecture — a decentralized autonomous AI system. The central thesis 
is that three independently validated design principles — forward-difference 
local learning (Error Diffusion, ED), unstructured peer-to-peer networking 
with metadata/payload separation, and intentional noise injection — can be 
combined to produce emergent collective intelligence without central 
coordination.

The architecture addresses three contemporary problems simultaneously:
(1) structural capture of AI computation by a small number of centralized 
platforms; (2) the filter bubble problem in AI agent systems; and 
(3) the suppression of serendipitous discovery as a structural consequence 
of algorithmic optimization.

We further propose a theory of Distributed Selfhood: that LLM nodes 
assigned persistent identifiers, accumulating local Body, and subjected 
to stochastic noise may exhibit emergent directional intentionality as 
a network.
